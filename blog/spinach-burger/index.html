Spinach Burger

When you buy cheap groceries you never know if the taste will be good or you just won’t like it and have to force yourself to eat it. But don’t worry, you can always make something else out of it and make it yummy! I didn’t like the taste of this frozen spinach so I decided to make spinach burgers and it turned out delicious!

Ingredients:

frozen spinach puree
can of chickpeas
oats
oat flour (optional)
spices (salt, pepper, curry, garlic, cayenne pepper)

Again, the exact measurements are not important, you just use what you have and make the best of it. Just make sure you don't use too much oats because it will be a bit blend. I used three times the amount shown in this picture.

Defrost the spinach in a pot on low heat or use freshly boiled and pureed spinach.

Blend the chickpeas or just crush them with a fork (you can leave a few big chunks).

Mix spinach, chickpeas and spices and then add oats (and oat flour) until you are able to make sticky patties. Be generous with spices because they make all the flavor! Try the mixture to see if it needs more spices.

You can make it a bit "too salty" and "too spicy" because it will make the taste stronger in the burger.

Wet your hands and make patties.

Put it on the baking paper and in the oven on 250°C / 482°F. Bake it for around 15 minutes - or just check until the surface has become dry and a bit crispy.

Serve it in the bun with (vegan) ketchup, mustard, diced onions amd mazve some ! Yum!
