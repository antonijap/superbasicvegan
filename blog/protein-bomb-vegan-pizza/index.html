Protein Bomb Vegan Pizza

If you like healthy but delicious food you will love this recipe! It's high in carbs and protein while low in fat and it contains no oil, sugar or yeast!

Ingredients:

flour (any kind is good but best if it's whole-grain)
water
baking powder
tomato sauce
beans (canned or cooked)
carrot
mushrooms
vegan pattie/ vegan salami/tofu (whichever you prefer)
vegan parmesan/vegan cheese
arugula

salt
oregano
smoked paprika
pepper

I didn't specify the amount of each ingredient because it depends solely on your preference of ingredients and how big pizza you are making.



Preheat the oven to around 200°C /392°F.

Mix flour and water with baking powder and a bit of salt and add water/flour until the consistency is wet but not runny, it shouldn't be as dry as regular pizza dough but more like cake dough.

Put it in a baking pan covered with baking paper and spread it evenly with wet hands.

Bake it until it starts looking firm but still pale cca 5 minutes.



Mix tomato sauce with a bit of salt, pepper, oregano and a half teaspoon of smoked paprika.

Grate the carrot and slice mushrooms and vegan pattie/tofu.

Take the dough out of the oven and cover it with previously made tomato sauce.

Put carrot, mushrooms, beans and pieces of vegan pattie/tofu on it and put it back in the oven.


Bake for 5-10 minutes (check when the dough gets golden on the edges and bottom).

When it's done cover it with vegan parmesan/vegan cheese and arugula.


Bon appetit!
