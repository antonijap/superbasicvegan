Banana Bread Apple Pie

Ingredients:

2 ripe bananas
2 tablespoons coconut oil (40g)
100g oat or wheat flour
1 tbs molasses / brown sugar
1 pack baking powder (5-10g)
4 medium apples
cinnamon
1 tbs brown sugar

Preheat oven to 200°C / 392°F.

Mix mashed bananas, oil, flour, molasses and baking powder in a bowl. Pour it in a pan covered with baking paper.

Grate apples and squeeze the juice out of them. Sprinkle cinnamon and brown sugar over apples and mix it together.

Put grated apples on top of batter in a pan and put it in the hot oven. Bake for 15-20 minutes (check for the bread to get golden).

That's it!

I recommend molasses over brown sugar but I didn't have any so I used sugar. If bananas and apples you are using are very sweet you can skip the sugar altogether!

This is definitely the cheapest and healthiest baked good you can imagine! And you can make it in the blink of an eye!

Enjoy!
